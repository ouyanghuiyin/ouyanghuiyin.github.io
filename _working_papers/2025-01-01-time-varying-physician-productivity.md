**Status:** Accepted at *Manufacturing & Service Operations Management*

**Authors:** Huiyin Ouyang, Ran Liu, Zhankun Sun

We study physician productivity in emergency departments, which varies within shifts due to shift-based scheduling, and develop a time-varying multi-server queueing model for ED staffing.
